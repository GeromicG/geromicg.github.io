Coding Challenge 3:Debating Inequality in the UK (WEEK 3)

Gini coefficient indicator depicts income inequality in the UK. As per Chart A, the trend has largely remained stable since the 1990s, though there has been a slight increase in the Gini coeefficient for all individuals(1.9 points) and retired individuals(3.9 points) in a ten year period from 2011/12 to 2021/22. But as per Chart B, in relative terms we can observe that UK's Gini coefficient is one of the highest among the OECD countries and does require policy intervention.
